Our Vision:

To pave the way towards an inclusive society.

Mission and Activities:

Arvestaran is dedicated to creating a safe and enabling environment where disadvantaged groups and the wider community build an inclusive and aware society.
The center provides a range of services, including consultancy and creative therapy services, educational programs, and capacity building for sector specialists. Arvestaran's Advocacy Hub promotes independence and creates job opportunities for individuals on the autism spectrum.

Outreach and Partnerships:

While based in Yerevan, Arvestaran continually extends its outreach to the regions and rural communities of Armenia. The center collaborates with local and international partners, including esteemed organizations like including the Armenian General Benevolent Union (AGBU), “Impact Hub Yerevan,” among others.

Sustainability and Support:

Currently financed by passionate supporters and founders, Arvestaran strives for sustainability and self-reliance. However, to address financial challenges exacerbated by the 2020 Artsakh war and the pandemic, Arvestaran is seeking support. Your organization's contribution can make a meaningful impact on the center's ability to continue providing crucial services.

How You Can Help:

We invite you to consider supporting Arvestaran through financial aid or by donating items and services.
Arvestaran needs around $3,000 monthly recurring donations, covering skill development programs, space rental, utility costs, equipment, and operational expenses. The center is seeking support from those who are keen to help disadvantaged and vulnerable groups.
Apart from financial aid, Arvestaran welcomes donations of various items and services, including woodworking tools, greenhouse equipment, kitchen equipment, books, laptops, and more. Support in the form of skill development training, vocational training, graphic design services, and media partnerships is also valued.

Beneficiary Impact:

Your support will empower individuals with autism and developmental disorders, fostering increased independence in their daily lives. From mastering essential life skills to making informed choices, our beneficiaries gain newfound confidence and autonomy. Through our inclusive programs, individuals on the spectrum develop and enhance their social skills. Your contribution helps create a supportive environment where they can build friendships, communicate effectively, and engage meaningfully with their peers and community.
